Getting Started

What services does StreamLineoffer?

StreamLine provides a comprehensive range of telecommunication services, including high-speed internet (Wi-Fi), mobile phone SIM packages, TV channels, and broadband solutions for both residential and business customers.

How do I choose the right plan for my needs?

Choosing the right plan depends on your usage. If you’re a light user, a basic plan might suffice. For heavy users or families, we recommend our unlimited or high-speed plans. You can also contact our customer service for personalized recommendations.

Can I bundle my services for a better deal?

Yes, StreamLine offers bundled packages that combine Wi-Fi, mobile, TV, and broadband services at discounted rates. Bundling your services can save you money and provide the convenience of managing everything under one account.

What should I do if I experience connectivity issues?

If you experience any connectivity issues, please contact our customer support team. We offer 24/7 support and will help you troubleshoot and resolve any problems as quickly as possible.

How do I manage my account and payments?

You can easily manage your account and payments through the StreamLine online portal or mobile app. View your usage, pay bills, and update your plan all in one convenient place.

Is there a contract or can I change my plan anytime?

StreamLine offers both contract-based and flexible, no-contract options. You can change your plan at any time based on your needs, ensuring you always have the best fit.
